About This Academy

Management is a process to achieve something that is done in groups by working together and utilizing all available resources. While the project can be interpreted as an activity that takes place within a certain period and by using limited resources to carry out predetermined tasks. Companies, organizations, or start-ups need to learn project management from managers and other HR so that the planned projects can meet the goals and achieve success.

With project management, it can be a powerful and very important tool for companies that can understand its use and have the potential to implement such project management. Very aggressive competition in the business environment makes project management seen as a tool that can help companies to carry out various designated projects efficiently and effectively. That is why any project environment will require project management.
