About Us

About Teknokrat!

Teknokrat Muda Indonesia positions itself as a space that connects young professional in the public, private, and civil society sectors in the science technology community. We are independent entity outside in the formal political and government structures. Our people wants to be part of realizing the Nation Building through the development of quality investment in science technology human resources. We believe that we can’t do it ourselves, we are also looking for any collaboration to learn, grown, and contribute together.

  • Be better and more advanced Indonesian youth
  • Contribute as part of the solution to the Nation's problems

Vision and Mission

“To be leading self-development platform For science technology community throughout Indonesia.”

  • Mission 1

    Equalizing competencies and qualifications of human resources throughout Indonesia in their field of expertise.

    Nationwide inclusive networks and peer support.
